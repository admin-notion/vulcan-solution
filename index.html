How we do it

We divide our projects into three phases and keep coming up with new ideas as your company grows. Creating enduring relationships with our clientele is another of our top priorities.

Design

We do a number of workshops with your team to better understand your project's goals, vision, and general company.

1. Recognize your business needs
2. Comprehend project requirements
3. Consult and advise
4. Wireframes
5. User and product flow
6. Design (UI & UX)
7. Prototype
8. Tech spec document

Development

We proceed to Stage 02 (Development) if you are happy with your Technical Specifications and Prototype (Stage 01).

1. Establish a plan for project development
2. Construct and code your project;
3. Design servers and architectur.
4. Make Test Plans
5. Verify the quality assurance
6. Conduct User Experiments
7. Plans for Acquiring Customers

Support

We will provide you an optional Maintenance & Support contract once your project is live to aid in the expansion of your company.

1. Evaluate data and trends
2. Maintain servers and architecture
3. Establish backup plans
4. Provide product updates
5. Add new features
6. Track customer behavior
7. Offer Growth Advice
8. Monitor Product Performance.
